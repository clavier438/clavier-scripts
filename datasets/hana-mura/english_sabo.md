### SABO
Our sabo, or tea room, is a special space for quiet reflection that invites you to connect with yourself and others through tea. We serve sencha, Kaga regional roasted tea, Japanese black tea, and original tea-based cocktails (chashu), all presented in thoughtfully crafted teaware created in collaboration with local artisans. Experience the spirit of chanoyu, Japan’s centuries-old tradition of hospitality, reimagined in a refined and contemporary style. At sabo, we offer a diverse menu starting with tea, alongside a selection of alcoholic beverages and sweets. Open to both ryokan guests and the general public. Plum Wine with Japanese Black Tea Sencha Gin & Tonic Matcha Beer Original Blended Tea Roasted Kaga Bōcha (Roasted Twig Tea) Local Ishikawa Black Tea Local Ishikawa Sencha Seasonal Fresh Tea Blend etc... RESERVE Enjoy a serene afternoon at Hanamurasaki with our exclusive Japanese-style afternoon tea.This thoughtfully curated experience features delicate vegetable sushi, seasonal wagashi (Japanese sweets), and a selection of premium Japanese teas. Each element is crafted to highlight the beauty of Japanese culinary tradition while offering a moment of pure relaxation. RESERVE
