### CUISINE
We offer a variety of meals that reflect the changing seasons and the spirit of Ishikawa Prefecture, thoughtfully prepared with carefully selected seasonal ingredients and artful tableware. In addition to authentic kaiseki cuisine, we also provide menu options catering to guests' dietary requirements and restrictions, along with tastefully curated pairings of sake and other beverages. Rooted in the traditions and philosophy of Japanese cuisine, each dining experience is elevated with a touch of contemporary creativity inviting you to discover a new expression of Japanese cuisine. Our head chef creates each menu with care, using the best seasonal ingredients. From appetisers to desserts, you can enjoy Japanese traditions alongside the fresh flavours of the local area. In winter, we welcome the arrival of Kanō crab, a celebrated delicacy of Ishikawa and a much-loved local variety of snow crab. Each guest is served the equivalent of one whole, fresh crab, carefully prepared using live crabs caught at the nearby fishing port, accompanied by a selection of seasonal dishes. It’s a special taste of the region we hope you’ll enjoy. This plan is available from 7th November to 20th March. Please note that due to adverse weather, the period may be subject to change, or crabs from other parts of the Sea of Japan may be used. Thank you for your understanding. Our menus highlight carefully selected seasonal vegetables and traditional local ingredients, such as Kaga vegetables, offering dishes that are also suitable for vegetarian guests. At Hanamurasaki, we do our utmost to accommodate guests with food allergies. While we are not able to completely guarantee allergen-free meals, we will take care to prepare dishes without the specified ingredients wherever possible. DETAIL We do our best to accommodate food allergies and dietary needs. However, please understand that making significant adjustments to the menu may incur an additional charge, and depending on the ingredients omitted, the number of dishes may be reduced. Japanese cuisine often relies on a variety of essential seasonings that can be difficult to remove completely. We appreciate your understanding in this regard. All meals are prepared in the same kitchen using shared equipment, including dishwashers and utensils. While we take great care, trace amounts of allergens may remain due to cross-contact during the cooking process. Because of this, we are unfortunately unable to guarantee a completely allergen-free environment. For guests with severe allergies, we may not be able to accommodate certain requests out of concern for your safety. We are happy to accommodate vegetarian, vegan, pescatarian, gluten-free, and other dietary preferences whenever possible. As same-day requests can be difficult to fulfil, we kindly ask that any dietary needs be shared with us when you book your stay. Please take a moment to review the above. If you have specific dietary concerns and would like to proceed, feel free to contact us by phone or include the details in the comment section of your reservation. We’ll be glad to assist you.
