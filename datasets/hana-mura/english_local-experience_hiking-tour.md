## ARTISAN TOURS
LOCAL EXPERIENCE TOUR PAGE Hiking in Satoyama Nature at Yamanaka Onsen Time : About 2 hours Price : ¥10,000 per person (transport extra, 30 min by taxi) Capacity : Up to 4 people Take a hike to the most nature-rich area of Yamanaka Onsen and explore the tiny, lovery village of Ōzuchi, home to just one resident. Designated as an Important Preservation District for Groups of Traditional Buildings, this area preserves the nostalgic landscapes of old Japan. Discover wild plants and mushrooms underfoot, admire the beautiful forests above, and enjoy the crystal-clear upstream river. This special tour takes you around the entire village, guided by the local resident, Mr. Nobo. Enjoy meaningful conversations with the local people.
