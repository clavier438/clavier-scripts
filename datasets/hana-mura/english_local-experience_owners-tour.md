## OWNER’S TOUR
LOCAL EXPERIENCE TOUR PAGE A Journey of Craft and Art with the Owner of Hanamurasaki. Time : Half day Price : ¥50,000 / person (transport and meals extra) Capacity : Up to 3 people (for guests staying 2 nights or more) Explore the hidden gems of Yamanaka Onsen and immerse yourself in Japanese aesthetics with Kohei Yamada, the sixth-generation owner of Hanamurasaki. From private visits to craftsman studios to discovering secluded scenic spots, Kohei, who developed a deep appreciation for Japanese culture after studying abroad, offers insider access to this unique community, where he has built strong connections with local farmers and artisans. This personalized tour highlights the beauty and craftsmanship of Yamanaka Onsen, reflected in the exceptional service and philosophy of Hanamurasaki. Tour Sample #1 SATOYAMA Visit a traditional rural landscape with just one resident and enjoy Japanese architecture and hiking on this unique tour. This is the most natural area in Yamanaka Onsen. Recognized as an Important Traditional Buildings Preservation District, this region still retains the nostalgic landscapes that allow you to feel the traditional Japanese way of life passed down through generations. This natural area features flowing spring water, which is the purest source in Yamanaka Onsen. You can see Japanese plants and rice paddies at your feet. The tea experience, brewed with the town's abundant water, promises to be an exceptional moment. Tour Sample #2 LOCAL ART PLACE A tour visiting the studios of local artists and craftsmen, featuring two to three locations. This area is where many young and talented artists live. By visiting their studios and hearing directly about the themes and inspirations behind their work, you'll gain a deeper appreciation for their creations. In Ishikawa Prefecture, you can find various arts and crafts, including Yamanaka lacquerware with a history of 400 years, Kutani pottery known for its diverse expressions, and Buddhist statues that have a deep connection to Japanese history.
