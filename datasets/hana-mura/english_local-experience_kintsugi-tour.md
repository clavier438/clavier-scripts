## ARTISAN TOURS
LOCAL EXPERIENCE TOUR PAGE Kintsugi Private Workshop Time : About 2 hours Price : ¥25,000 per person (transport extra , 20 minutes by taxi) Capacity : Up to 4 people This specialized Kintsugi workshop receives repair requests from all over the country. Shigeki Yagi, the Kintsugi master, handles repairs not only for Japanese antiques but also for items like old Baccarat glass. He has also presented as a Kintsugi artist at FIAT's 120th anniversary event, "FIAT x MADE IN JAPAN PROJECT." This workshop offers a special tour where participants can learn Kintsugi directly from Yagi himself, and you can take home your repaired piece. You can take home the kintsugi pieces you created after the experience. Kintsugi turns broken pottery into beautiful art, celebrating its unique story.
