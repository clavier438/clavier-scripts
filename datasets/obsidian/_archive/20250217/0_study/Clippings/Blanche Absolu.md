Blanche is a floral fragrance from the Byredo luxury fragrance collection, inspired by the contrasts of texture and fabric and skin. Black pepper pops against neroli and aldehydic, accentuating the bold floral heart of rose absolue, violet, and watery jasmine. Anchored by a base of musk and amber wood, the scent deepens into cashmere wood and dry cashmeran.
