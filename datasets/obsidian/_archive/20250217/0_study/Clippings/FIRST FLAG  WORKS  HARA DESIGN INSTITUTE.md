## FIRST FLAG

FIRST FLAG is a brand of knit items that has leveraged the delicate handwork of Suzhou, China, known for its embroidery. These are Chinese children's clothes whose silk and cotton materials are chosen carefully and can easily be labeled world-class top quality. The supple texture of fine silk; cotton sourced from around the world, with a strict eye to securing safety; handicrafts born of proficient weaving skills: witnessing these, we offered to help these values advance as a brand. We handled integrated brand design, including the symbol mark, packaging and shop design.

### 1\. Product Design: A world of textured white

Going beyond mere functionality in a swaddling cloth for newborns, we hit upon the idea of “festival garb”. Combining China’s art of exquisite hand knitting with high quality materials, we developed a “world of white”, rich with purity. Fashion designer Kousuke Tsumura is responsible for the design.
